The Mission of Music Resurrectus

The purpose of Music Resurrectus is to remind listeners of the emotions and vibes they felt when listening to their favorite song. To remind them of the talent they admired, idolized and loved in their favorite artist. We want to resurrect the value, passion and creativity that has been lost in most current songs and artists.
